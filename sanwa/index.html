About us

Sanwa is one of the pioneer manufacturers of Diamond Tools in India, with experience of more than two decades. Established in the year 1994, the company's efforts have been directed towards providing “Total Solutions” in the field of Stone and Concrete cutting.

Sanwa's growth as a National Organisation has been based on the solid foundation of its research and technical expertise, commitment to its customers in the quality and deliveries and unmatched response to customer's needs.
